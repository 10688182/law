FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Common Legal Questions

Find answers to some of our most frequently asked legal questions.

We offer a free 30-minute initial consultation for most legal matters. During this consultation, we'll discuss your situation and provide guidance on how we can assist you. For complex matters requiring specialized expertise, we may charge a nominal fee which will be disclosed upfront.

For your first meeting, please bring any relevant documents related to your legal matter, including contracts, court papers, or correspondence. If your matter involves financial aspects, bring relevant financial statements or records. Our team will provide you with a specific list of documents to bring based on your particular legal needs.

Our fee structure varies depending on the type of legal service. We offer hourly rates, fixed fees for certain services, and in some cases, contingency fees (where we only get paid if you win your case). During your initial consultation, we'll discuss the fee structure that applies to your specific matter and provide a clear estimate of anticipated costs.

The duration depends on the complexity of your case and whether it involves court proceedings. Some matters can be resolved in weeks, while complex litigation may take months or years. After reviewing your situation, we'll provide an estimated timeline and keep you informed of progress throughout the process.

Yes, we offer secure video consultations for clients who prefer not to visit our office in person. Our virtual consultations maintain the same level of confidentiality as in-person meetings. We use encrypted platforms to ensure your privacy is protected.

With over 25 years of experience, Justice Chambers combines deep legal expertise with a client-centered approach. Unlike larger firms where clients can feel like just another case number, we provide personalized attention from experienced attorneys. Our commitment to excellence and ethical practice has earned us recognition from prestigious legal organizations.

The strength of a legal case depends on many factors including evidence, applicable laws, and specific circumstances. During your consultation, we'll evaluate the merits of your case and provide honest advice about your legal options and likelihood of success. We'll never take on a case unless we believe we can provide meaningful help.

For urgent legal matters, please call our main office number immediately. If your call is outside regular business hours, our answering service will connect you with the attorney on call. In emergency situations involving imminent legal action (such as arrests or restraining orders), we can arrange for immediate representation.
