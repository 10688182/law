OUR HISTORY

Our Legal Legacy

From humble beginnings to becoming a trusted legal authority

1995

Firm Established

Justice Chambers was founded by Elizabeth Carter with just two attorneys focusing on civil litigation and family law. Our first office opened in downtown with a commitment to accessible, high-quality legal services.

2002

Expansion to Corporate Law

Recognizing the growing need for business legal services, we expanded our practice to include corporate law, adding three new attorneys with expertise in business formation, contracts, and commercial transactions.

2008

New Headquarters

We moved to our current state-of-the-art offices, allowing us to better serve our growing client base. This year also marked our first recognition in the "Best Law Firms" ranking.

2015

Technology Integration

We implemented cutting-edge legal technology to enhance client service, including secure client portals, electronic document management, and virtual consultation capabilities.

2023

Comprehensive Legal Services

Today, Justice Chambers offers full-service legal representation across five core practice areas with a team of 15 experienced attorneys. We continue to grow while maintaining our commitment to personalized service and legal excellence.
