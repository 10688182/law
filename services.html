Corporate Law

Our corporate law practice provides comprehensive legal services to businesses of all sizes, from startups to multinational corporations. We offer strategic counsel on corporate governance, compliance, mergers & acquisitions, and commercial transactions to help clients navigate complex business environments.

Our corporate law services include:

  • Business formation and corporate structuring

  • Mergers, acquisitions, and divestitures

  • Corporate governance and compliance

  • Private equity and venture capital transactions

  • Securities law and capital raising

  • Commercial contracts and transactions

Real Estate Law

Our real estate practice handles all aspects of property law, from residential transactions to complex commercial developments. We provide comprehensive services including due diligence, zoning, financing, and dispute resolution. Our team helps clients navigate the complexities of real estate transactions while protecting their interests at every stage.

  • Residential and commercial property transactions

  • Real estate financing and development

  • Land use and zoning matters

  • Lease agreements and landlord-tenant disputes

  • Title examinations and insurance

  • Real estate litigation and dispute resolution

Intellectual Property

We handle patents, trademarks, copyrights, and trade secrets with technical expertise across various industries. From registration to enforcement, we provide comprehensive IP services to safeguard your innovations and creative works.

  • Patent prosecution and litigation

  • Trademark registration and enforcement

  • Copyright protection and licensing

  • Trade secret protection and litigation

  • IP portfolio management and strategy

  • Technology transfer and licensing agreements

Family Law

Our family law attorneys handle sensitive matters with compassion and discretion. We provide guidance on divorce, child custody, adoption, and prenuptial agreements, always prioritizing our clients' best interests. Whether through negotiation or litigation, we strive to achieve favorable outcomes while minimizing emotional and financial stress.

  • Divorce and separation proceedings

  • Child custody and visitation matters

  • Child and spousal support

  • Prenuptial and postnuptial agreements

  • Adoption and guardianship proceedings

  • Domestic violence protection orders

Contact Our Family Law Team

Employment Law

Our employment law practice provides comprehensive counsel to both employers and employees on workplace legal matters. We help businesses establish compliant policies and procedures, while protecting employees' rights in disputes. Our attorneys are skilled in navigating the complex web of employment laws and regulations.

  • Employment contracts and agreements

  • Workplace discrimination and harassment claims

  • Wage and hour compliance

  • Wrongful termination claims

  • Employee handbooks and policies

  • Non-compete and trade secret disputes
